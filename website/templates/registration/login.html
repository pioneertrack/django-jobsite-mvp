What is BearFounders?

Bear Founders is designed to help create startup connections. No longer do people have to post ads on Facebook, ask their friends to ask their friends, or post paper flyers on bulletin boards. Bear Founders exclusively allows Berkeley students and Alumni to create profiles for either a startup or an individual.

Search

Search for people based on their name, year, major, or skillset. Find people who fit your needs and have the experience you require. Search for startups who are hiring based on name, industry, and positions available

Connect

After you think you have found the right match, send a connection request. This will email the user saying that there is an interest in their profile. Give them more information about yourself and what you are hiring/looking for

Build

After both parties reach an agreement on the pay/partnership, start working on your project together! It's quick and easy.

Having issues signing up? Email help@bearfounders.com
